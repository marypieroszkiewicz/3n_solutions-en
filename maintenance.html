​Maintenance of electrical equipment

Maintenance of electrical devices is an essential element of proper maintenance in production plants. Our technology allows for maintenance without downtime in production, is ecological and safe, and also extends the life of components.

We divide maintenance work into three basic stages:

  • Preparation
  • Maintenance
  • Termination

Site inspection - inventory of devices indicated by the customer, preliminary technical condition assessment.

Thermovision and electrical measurements before performing maintenance - detailed assessment of the condition of electrical components and devices and possible threats inspection report - containing a summary of Stages 1 and 2.
