After-fire cleaning

We have the skills and experience, supported by large orders, in removing the effects of fires. Savings obtained using our technology can reach very significant sums of money. The use of 3NS methods eliminates the need to replace up to 90% of components.

Time required to remove the downtime caused by fire is very short. The components can be restored to their functional state in less time than replacement of burnt wiring would require.

The dry gas mixture method used allows 100% removal of soot from the components. The method also counteracts corrosion. 3NS does not utilize water, which, in reaction with halogen compounds, a by-product of insulation combustion, causes corrosion of plastic, copper and aluminium components.

In addition to cleaning and repair recovery and repairs, ozone treatment which disinfects files and leaves the smell of soot.
